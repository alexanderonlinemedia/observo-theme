== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance -> Themes and click the Add New button.
2. Click Upload and Choose File, then select the theme's ZIP file. Click Install Now.
3. Click Activate to use your new theme right away.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I use a header logo? =

You can change the title area to feature your logo.
** Recommended Size: 520px width or larger **
** We also recomend using a .SVG format for your logo **

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance -> Observo Options.
2. Now you will see the 'WordSkins Admin Panel' and a tab called 'General Settings' will already be selected.
3. Click the 'Logo Settings' tab to reveal the Header Logo setting.
4. Here you can enter a URL or click the 'Upload Image' button to open the WordPress Media Library.
5. Either select your previously uploaded logo or click the 'Upload Files' tab and upload your image.
6. Click the 'Choose Image' button. Your logo's URL will be added to the 'Header Logo' input.
7. Click the 'Save Changes' button in the 'WordSkins Admin Panel'.

= How do I add the Social Links to the footer? =

Observo allows you to display links to your social media profiles, like Twitter and Facebook, with icons.

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance -> Observo Options.
2. You will see the 'WordSkins Admin Panel' and a tab called 'General Settings' will be already selected.
3. Click the 'Social Media Settings' tab to reveal the social icons settings.
4. Click the show/hide button for each social network that you would like to display.
5. Add your social media profile URLs for each icon that you would like to display.
6. Click the 'Save Changes' button in the 'WordSkins Admin Panel'.

Available icons:

* Facebook
* Twitter
* Google+
* LinkedIn
* Instagram
* Pinterest
* Youtube
* Vimeo
* Dribbble
* Behance
